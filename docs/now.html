What I’m working on

BRASS Digital Lab

I am the founder and director of the BRASS Digital Lab — the first and only Regulatory Technology (RegTech) and Supervisory Technology (SupTech) firm covering all 76+ UAE accredited higher education institutions.

The Lab currently operates 4 live compliance platforms, with 20 completed builds and 52 in active development — serving universities (Edu-RegTech: institutional compliance management) and government bodies (Edu-SupTech: real-time sector-wide oversight for MoHESR and CAA).

Research

TipDefining Question

How does the transition to cognitive-capital-intensive production reshape economic value creation, distributional outcomes, and the boundaries of firms, markets, and states?

All working papers are hosted on the BRASS Digital Lab Research Portal — 27 papers across 6 research programmes. Active projects include:

Inference Economics:

  • The Inference Economy: Token Consumption, Software Productivity, and Jevons Paradox Dynamics in AI — Formalizing the Token Production Function, Token Kuznets Curve, and Jevons Paradox of AI Tokens with calibrated simulation evidence.
  • Tokens as Technology: AI Inference, Labor Augmentation, and Long-Run Software Productivity Growth — Macroeconomic growth model treating AI inference tokens as Harrod-neutral labor-augmenting technology.
  • The Transformative Role of MCP in the AI-Driven API Economy — DiD study on 10,000 simulated firms showing MCP reduces integration costs by ~31%.

Agentic Innovation Economics:

  • Agentic Innovation Economics: Autonomous AI Agents and the Future of Endogenous Technological Change — Formal theoretical framework extending endogenous growth theory to autonomous AI innovators.
  • A Complete Formal Endogenous Growth Model with Autonomous AI Innovation Agents — Full equilibrium analysis and simulation.
  • AI’s Impact on Fresh Graduate Labor Demand — CES production framework + Monte Carlo, IEEE conference paper.

OBEF / RegTech Research:

  • Regulation-Induced Innovation in UAE Higher Education — Formal model and Monte Carlo simulation of the Edu-RegTech market emerging from the MoHESR OBF mandate.
  • Digital Fair Trade Platforms for UAE HE Regulation — Platform-economics model integrating mechanism design and Monte Carlo calibration.

Digitalization, Inclusion & Development:

  • Open Banking Maturity, Financial Inclusion, and Firm Productivity: Global Evidence
  • Digital and Financial Inclusion in Burkina Faso: Household Welfare and COVID-19
  • Trading in Digital Financial Markets: Retail Investment Demand in the UAE
